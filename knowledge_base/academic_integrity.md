# Academic Integrity Policy (Summary)

## Principles

Academic integrity is founded upon:

- Honesty
- Trust
- Fairness
- Respect
- Responsibility

## Misconduct Includes

- Plagiarism
- Contract cheating
- Fabrication of data
- Unauthorized AI usage when prohibited

## Responsible AI Use

Where AI tools are permitted:

- Students must acknowledge use.
- AI output must not replace original intellectual contribution.
- Generated content must be critically evaluated.

## Investigation Procedure

- Initial academic review
- Student explanation opportunity
- Escalation to misconduct panel if necessary
