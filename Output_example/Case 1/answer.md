# Answer

Donald Trump is an American businessman, television personality, and politician who served as the 45th President of the United States from 2017 to 2021.

He was born on June 14, 1946, in Queens, New York. Before entering politics, he built a business empire through his real estate company, The Trump Organization.

Trump's presidency was marked by controversy and polarization, with policies such as tax cuts, deregulation, and a hardline stance on immigration. He also faced numerous investigations and scandals during his time in office.

Some key facts about Donald Trump:

* He has written several bestselling books, including "The Art of the Deal" and "Great Again: How to Fix Our Crippled America".
* He hosted the reality TV show "The Apprentice" from 2004 to 2015.
* He was a vocal supporter of Brexit during the 2016 UK referendum.
* He has been awarded several honorary degrees, including one from Liberty University.
